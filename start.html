Welcome to Reno, Nice 'n' Cool

Welcome to Reno, Nice 'n' Cool. I am proud to showcase my pictures from my trip to Reno and share with all of you my story of the trip. To view the pictures, click on the links below. Before then, I'll start with my Prelude.

Driving through Oregon for about 5 hours was, as expected, quite typical and mundane. Having seen essentially all of I-5 in Oregon, I knew mostly what to expect, and the weather was quite cloudy, with some rain from a little in Cottage Grove to quite a bit in Roseburg. Once I reached Medford and Ashland, I saw some sun peeking through the clouds. Not surprisingly, after heading up and down the rollercoaster that is known as Siskiyou Pass (the highest point along I-5), I was in California, and of course, the clouds virtually disappeared like that (a few scattered, but still, a different world).
